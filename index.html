About Me

Prosthetic Hand Design - Autodesk Inventor

Students apart of McMaster's ENG I program were able to design a prosthetic hand for a cerebral palsy patient. A design process consisted of a multiple drawings, which were then finalized and produced using Inventor by using 3D design as well as gear animation. The constraints present in the challenge were that the gear-train based mechanism has to be powered by a motor and obtain a specified output speed of 0.1 RPS with a given input speed of 20.4 RPM.
